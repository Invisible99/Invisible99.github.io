Salty Sands

This is a hack’n’slash game for up to four players where the goal is to be the last person standing.

C#/ Visual Studio/ Unity

This is a project that I made together with two other programmers and two artists in Unity. It is an offline couch party game. Every play picks a different class that they will use. Waves of undead will try and kill you, but watch out, other players can also kill you to stop you of becoming the boss. After the waves are over the player with the most kills will become the last enemy. To help with the waves pickups will spawn but be careful picking them up the beast is protecting them.

What did I do?? I worked on a few things. I worked on the different classes and did the dashes and abilities. I added all the UI to the game and made it work. There are different kind of game modes I did the full PvP game mode. I added the the pick up system to the game as well. There are other little things that I worked on or that I helped with like the camera movement and movement of the classes.

To play this game you need at least one controller to play alone (maximum of four controllers if you play with more).
