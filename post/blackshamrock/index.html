Black Shamrock

I first started as an intern at Black Shamrock where I worked mostly on Paranoia: Happiness is Mandatory but got moved to an undisclosed game.

Paranoia: Happiness is Mandatory gave me insight in how to better work with Unity and in bigger team.

The undisclosed multiplayer VR game was my first work experience with Unreal Engine 4 and VR. I converted blueprints to code and made it all work in the multiplayer setting.

After my internship they hired me full time where I worked on titles suchs as Jedi: Fallen Order and Destroy All Humans. I also worked on a small inhouse project.

For Jedi: Fallen Order I worked on the integrating the new UI. In Destroy All Humans I worked on the AI of 3 bosses of the game. I can't say much about the small inhouse project but I learened more about changing the UI of Unreal Engine and working with Stadia.
